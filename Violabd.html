Google Sheets Setup

To connect to your Google Sheet:

  1. Create a Google Sheet with columns: Name, Date, Project, Tasks, Hours, Status, Notes
  2. Click "Share" and set to "Anyone with the link can view"
  3. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL (the long string between /d/ and /edit)
  4. Paste your Sheet ID below and click "Load Data"
